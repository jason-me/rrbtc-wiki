## Mobile Device Privacy Threats
> "We kill people based on metadata." — Fmr. NSA Spy Chief Michael Hayden
- Government Surveillance: Section 702, PRISM, xkeyscore
- COVID-19 Contact Tracing built into iOS/Android at OS level (2020)
- Carrier Data-Mining: AT&T lawsuits regarding sale of location data
- Google Connectivity Checks: constant phoning home even on idle devices

## Actionable Privacy Tools
### Immediate Steps
- VPNs: Mullvad, ExpressVPN
- Burner SIMs: cash pay-as-you-go, refill via Bitrefill, Silent Link (anonymous eSIM)
- Encrypted Messaging: Signal, Telegram (Secret Chats), Matrix/Element, ProtonMail
- OS Maintenance: turn off location sharing, review app permissions, delete unused apps

### Privacy-Focused Operating Systems
| OS | Features | Cost |
| GrapheneOS | Fully open source, privacy by default, sandboxed Google Play | Free |
| CalyxOS | Based on AOSP, microG, pre-installed privacy tools | Free |
| Copperhead OS | Managed secure vault, enterprise support | ~$250/3mo |
