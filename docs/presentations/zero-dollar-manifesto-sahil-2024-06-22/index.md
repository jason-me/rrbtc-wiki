# Zero Dollar Manifesto
**Presenter:** Sahil Chaturvedi
**Event:** Round Rock Bitcoiners #24
**Date:** June 22, 2024

## Overview
Personal finance philosophy advocating for minimizing fiat currency holdings in favor of maximizing Bitcoin accumulation. Not about extreme austerity: it supports gradual, incremental transition toward holding primarily sats while still owning other possessions and assets.

## Why Get On Zero
| Problem | Cause |
|---------|-------|
| Diminishing purchasing power | Fiat inflation |
| Unreliable long-term wealth preservation | Structural flaws in government money |
| Playing hot potato with weak money | Forced velocity due to debasement |

Bitcoin's sound money properties: scarce, portable, divisible, durable, fungible, private.

## Core Philosophy
- **Not a purity test.** Gradual transition is valid. No gatekeeping on the timeline.
- **Upgrade, not sacrifice.** Replacing fiat with Bitcoin is moving to a superior money, not depriving yourself.
- **Freedom to spend (not sell).** When you upgrade your money, spending bitcoin goes from ideological to logical.

## Transition Strategy
1. **DCA & dip buying** to navigate volatility during accumulation
2. **Organic over time** rather than sudden conversion
3. **Dollar as legacy payment rail** rather than store of value

## Operational Toolkit
| Function | Tool Type |
|----------|-----------|
| Fiat on/off ramps | ACH node (bank transfer conduit) |
| Conversion | Bitcoin exchange (Bisq, Strike, Bitrefill) |
| Daily spending | Bitcoin-linked debit/credit card |
| Compliance | Tax software for Bitcoin transactions |

## Key Quotes
> Dollars are a legacy payment rail to move bitcoin.

> The greatest gift bitcoin could give you is to stop thinking about bitcoin.
> -- @ProgrammableTx

> When you upgrade your money, spending bitcoin goes from being ideological to logical.

## Call to Action
> Upgrade your dollars to bitcoin. #GetOnZero
