# Cashu Mints & Local Bitcoin Adoption: Meetup #10 Summary
**Date:** March 9, 2023

## Community

## Onboarding Local Businesses
- Goal: help businesses avoid credit card fees (1.5-3.5%)
- Active Local Markets: Farmer George Market, Wolf Ranch Farmers Market, Texas Farmer's Market at Lakeline, Wilco Farmer's Market

### Payment Tools
- Oshi App: marketing and rewards using Bitcoin instead of points
- IbexPay: instant Lightning and On-Chain for businesses
- OpenNode: API and e-commerce plugins
- Strike & Cash App: consumer-friendly
- BTCMap: open-source project tagging Bitcoin-accepting locations

## Cashu & Chaumian eCash
> "Cashu is a free and open-source Chaumian ecash system built for Bitcoin. Nobody needs to know who you are, how much funds you have, and with whom you transact."
- Based on David Chaum's 1982 Blinded Signature Technology
- Wallet Example: Nutstash (wallet.nutstash.app)

### Privacy via Mint Swaps
- Internal transactions within a single mint: perfect privacy
- Inter-mint swaps: receiving mint doesn't know origin
- Using different wallets/nodes for deposit and redemption erases traceability at low cost (only LN fees)

## Practical Exercise: eCash over Nostr
1. Create wallet at wallet.nutstash.app
2. Add a mint
3. Configure Nostr settings to enable receiving tokens directly
