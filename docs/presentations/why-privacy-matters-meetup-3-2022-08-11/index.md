## Outline & Notes

# Why Privacy Matters — Meetup #3 (2022-08-11)

## Why Privacy Matters
- Identity fraud cost Americans ~$56 billion in 2020 (49 million consumers)
- NSA xkeyscore program revealed by Snowden (still active)
- Operation Chokepoint, Canadian Truckers
> "Selectively Reveal Your Data. Control what parts of you that you want to reveal."

## How to Obtain No-KYC Bitcoin
| Method | Details |
| Bitcoin ATMs | CoinATMradar.com; anonymous pre-paid phone; fees 8-20% |
| Bisq Network | Open-source DEX, multisig escrow, own node, Cash by Mail |
| Peer to Peer | Meetups, buy from friend |
| Azteco Vouchers | 5% markup (1.5% seller, 3.5% Azteco) |
| Mining (at home) | Pay through electric provider; up-front miner investment |

## Wallet Recommendations
### Mobile (Hot)
- Samourai — on-chain, privacy focused
- Blockstream Green — on-chain, HWI, multisig
- Blue Wallet — on-chain, Lightning, own node
- Muun Wallet — on-chain, Lightning

### Hardware (Cold)
- Trezor — open-source, non-custodial
- Ledger — closed-source, HSM
- ColdCard — verifiable-source, air-gapped, secure elements
- Seed Signer — fully open-source, DIY

## Forward-Looking Privacy Practices
- VPN/Tor: Mullvad, ExpressVPN, TOR browser
- Coinjoin: Whirlpool, JoinMarket, Wasabi
- Lightning: Bolt-12, Payjoin, Stowaway, PayNyms, BIP-47
- UTXO Management: never reuse addresses, Stonewall
