# Zero Dollar Manifesto
**Presenter:** Sahil Chaturvedi
**Date:** June 22, 2024
**Meetup:** Round Rock Bitcoiners #24
**Deck:** https://speakerdeck.com/rrbtc/zero-dollar-manifesto-sahil-chaturvedi

## Slide Outline

1. **Title** - Zero Dollar Manifesto: The case for holding zero fiat
2. **Bitcoin Sound Money** - Scarce, portable, divisible, durable, fungible, private
3. **Fiat Framing** - Dollars as legacy payment rail to move bitcoin; playing hot potato with weak money
4. **Why Now** - Diminishing purchasing power, structural flaws in government money
5. **Clarification** - Not a purity test; gradual transition is valid
6. **Transition Strategy** - DCA, buying dips, organic over time
7. **Philosophy** - Beauty in simplicity: the goal is to stop thinking about bitcoin as an investment
8. **Spending Framework** - Upgrade your money, spending becomes logical not ideological
9. **Operational Toolkit** - ACH node, exchange, card, tax software
10. **Call to Action** - Upgrade your dollars to bitcoin. #GetOnZero

## Key Takeaways
- GetOnZero is a personal finance philosophy, not an austerity movement
- Bitcoin is the upgrade; fiat is the legacy payment rail
- Gradual DCA and dip buying smooth the volatility of transition
- The end state: spending bitcoin is logical, not ideological
