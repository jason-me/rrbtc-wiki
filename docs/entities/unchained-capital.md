# Unchained Capital

Collaborative custody provider and financial services company focused on bitcoin-native products.

## Model

- **2-of-3 multisig** — You hold 2 keys, Unchained holds 1
- You retain sovereign control; Unchained cannot spend without your signature
- If you lose a key, Unchained can help recover with their key + your remaining key

## Services

- Collaborative custody vaults
- Bitcoin-backed loans
- Retirement accounts (Bitcoin IRA)
- Inheritance planning
- Retirement calculator tools

## Differentiation

- Financial services integration (loans, IRAs) alongside custody
- Key recovery service without taking full custody
- Open-source tools and educational content
