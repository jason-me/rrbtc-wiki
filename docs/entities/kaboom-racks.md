# KaboomRacks

ASIC mining hardware marketplace. Sells refurbished and used miners, often at prices accessible to home miners.

## Role in Home Mining
- Recommended by Round Rock Bitcoiners as a source for "next gen ASICs"
- Bear-market pricing makes hobby mining viable: S19J Pro+ 120Th units at approximately $2,000
- Reduces the capital barrier for new miners compared to new-equipment distributors

## Comparison to Retail
| Source | Pricing | Best For |
|--------|---------|----------|
| KaboomRacks (used/refurb) | Lower | Home miners on a budget |
| Direct manufacturer | Higher, latest models | Industrial/scale operations |

## Mining Hardware Mentioned
- S19J Pro+ (120Th): common entry-level ASIC for home setups
