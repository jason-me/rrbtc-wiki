# ShushMiner

Manufacturer of sound-dampening enclosures for Bitcoin ASIC miners. Targets residential miners who need to reduce noise without custom fabrication.

## Positioning
- Commercial alternative to DIY home builds (e.g., Econoalchemist guide)
- Competes with Upstream Data Ohmm BlackBox in the residential enclosure market
- Enclosures start around $1,700 and scale up with capacity and features

## Comparison to Alternatives
| Option | Price Range | Best For |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| ShushMiner | ~$1,700+ | Pre-built, plug-and-play noise reduction |
| Upstream Ohmm BlackBox | ~$2,500+ | Higher-end, weather-resistant builds |
| DIY BlackBox | Variable materials | Tinkerers, budget miners |
| Immersion kits (FogHashing, MineStack) | $2,000+ | Hot climates, maximum noise reduction |
