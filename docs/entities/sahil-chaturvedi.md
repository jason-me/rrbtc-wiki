# Sahil Chaturvedi

Bitcoiner, designer, and advocate for the #GetOnZero philosophy. Presented the Zero Dollar Manifesto at Round Rock Bitcoiners Meetup #24.

## Background
- **Website:** [sahilc.com](https://sahilc.com)
- **Company:** STUDIO TWENTYONE
- **Twitter:** [@SahilC0](https://twitter.com/SahilC0)
- **Nostr:** sahil@NostrVerified.com

## Contributions
- Authored the Zero Dollar Manifesto: a framework for transitioning from fiat to Bitcoin as primary money
- Advocates for gradual, non-purist transition to Bitcoin-only living
- Emphasizes Bitcoin as an upgrade to money rather than an investment to obsess over

## Philosophy
> The greatest gift bitcoin could give you is to stop thinking about bitcoin.
> -- @ProgrammableTx (quoted by Sahil)
