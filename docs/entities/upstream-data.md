# Upstream Data

Bitcoin mining infrastructure company founded by Steve Barbour. Best known for the Ohmm BlackBox: a sound-attenuated, weather-resistant enclosure for ASIC miners.

## Products
- **Ohmm BlackBox**: Turn-key mining enclosure with integrated cooling, filtration, and noise dampening
  - Commercial units start around $2,500
  - Designed for residential and small commercial deployments
  - Reduces noise and heat exhaust to neighbor-friendly levels
- **Upstream Data**: Also builds modular mining infrastructure for oil-and-gas sites, using stranded natural gas as power source

## Role in Home Mining
- Commercial enclosures are expensive ($2,500+) compared to DIY builds
- Round Rock Bitcoiners have seen used BlackBoxes offered for as low as $500 + shipping in secondary markets
- Alternative to DIY builds (e.g., Econoalchemist guide) for miners wanting a finished product

## Nakamoto Forum Connection
Steve Barbour (Upstream Data founder) debated Jesse Peltan (HODL Ranch) at the Bitcoin Commons in Austin on whether hydrocarbons are the most sustainable energy source for Bitcoin mining.
