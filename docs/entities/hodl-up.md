# Hodl Up

Bitcoin mining-themed board game. Teaches proof-of-work concepts, mining economics, and hodling strategy through physical gameplay.

## Purpose
- Educational tool for introducing Bitcoin concepts to newcomers in a low-stakes, social setting
- Covers mining difficulty, block rewards, and the trade-offs between selling and holding
- Family-friendly format suitable for meetup game nights

## Why It Appears in the Wiki
- Featured by Round Rock Bitcoiners as a way to make Bitcoin education interactive
- Complements more technical presentations with hands-on learning

## Acquisition
Available for purchase via the game's official website or Bitcoin-only merchants.
