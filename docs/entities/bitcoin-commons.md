# Bitcoin Commons

Community space and event venue in Austin, Texas, focused on Bitcoin education, networking, and public discourse.

## Location
601 Congress Avenue, Suite 250, Austin, TX 78701
(Second floor of the Littlefield Building; lobby entrance on East 6th Street next to Capital One Cafe)

## Activities
- Hosts the Nakamoto Forum Debate Series: quarterly structured debates on Bitcoin-centric topics
- Provides meeting space for local Bitcoin meetups and workshops
- Dinner-and-discussion format for evening events

## Notable Events
- **Nakamoto Forum Debate (April 2023)**: Steve Barbour (Upstream Data) vs Jesse Peltan (HODL Ranch), moderated by Tuur Demeester
  - Resolution: "Hydrocarbons are the most sustainable source of energy to mine bitcoin"

## Relation to Round Rock Bitcoiners
- Austin-area Bitcoin hub; Round Rock Bitcoiners members have attended Commons events
- Serves as a model for community infrastructure and structured debate formats
