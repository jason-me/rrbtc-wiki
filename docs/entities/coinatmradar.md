# CoinATMRadar

Directory and map of Bitcoin ATMs worldwide. Use to locate nearby machines for cash-to-bitcoin purchases.

## Privacy Tips
- Use an anonymous prepaid phone for SMS verification (if required by the machine operator)
- Buy small amounts to stay below KYC thresholds
- Be aware that many ATMs have cameras; consider your local threat model

## Trade-offs
| Advantage | Limitation |
|-----------|------------|
| Immediate cash-to-bitcoin | High fees (8-20%) |
| No bank account required | KYC thresholds vary by operator and jurisdiction |
| Wide geographic coverage | Rates and availability change frequently |
