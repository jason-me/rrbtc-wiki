# Welcome to the Round Rock Bitcoiners Wiki

Over the last 4 years we have hosted numerous talks and workshops from both within the Round Rock community and outside from the greater Central Texas region. We are very blessed to be positioned so close to the Bitcoin mecca and want to preserve that content in a way that is properly indexed, vetted, and easily searchable.

If you ever missed a meetup you can now search that content from the last 4 years in our community knowledge base and are welcome to contribute through GitHub or Radicle.

Thanks to our fellow bitcoiners in the greater Austin area for helping make this community what it is today. Thank you for sharing your knowledge for others to learn and grow from. We would like to preserve this content for future bitcoiners, no-coiners, and anyone in between.

You make this community building exercise both a rewarding and worthy endeavor by helping to bring about education around the amazing Bitcoin and freedom tech tools that we have at our disposal today.

This is just the beginning. We hope to grow this knowledge base in the months and years to come.
