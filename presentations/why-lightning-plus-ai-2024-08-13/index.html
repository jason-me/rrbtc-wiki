Why Lightning + AI (Meetup #27)

Date: 2024-08-08
Presenter: RRBTC
Topics: lightning, ai, casdcr, plebdevs

Alternate Deck Link: Published CASCDR Deck on SpeakerDeck

Outline & Notes​

Summary​

Lightning Network crash course + AI/LLM overview + how the CASCDR platform brings them together.

Lightning Crash Course​

  • Channels-based payment network built on Bitcoin
  • Peers lock up bitcoin UTXOs in shared channels
  • Payments routed between existing channels
  • Analogies: bar tabs, abacus beads
  • Advantages: low fees, high resolution (small amounts), instant, cryptographically provable, built-in privacy

AI / LLM Crash Course​

  • Decades of research culminating in widespread adoption
  • Rule-based programming has limitations: poor language/vision recognition, brittle
  • Neural networks overcome these limitations
  • Scaling applied to the entire internet produced human-like language understanding
  • "Under the hood: It's really just autocorrect but better!"
  • Key concept: "We've Quantized Language and Can Make It Into Math!"

AI Use Cases​

  • Customer service
  • Legal services
  • Code writing
  • Translation
  • Surveillance & psyops
  • Cross-industry productivity gains

AI Risks​

  • Privacy: surveillance at massive scale
  • Centralization: regulatory capture + data/tool hoarding by large entities
  • Antisocial use: manipulation and harm

Bitcoin's Role​

Bitcoin is a MAJOR tool in helping us solve these problems

"Use AI and bitcoin tools to empower the individual. To help users get the most out of the productivity gains from AI and to enrich their lives while preserving their privacy and respecting their beliefs, wishes and sovereignty. Furthermore, we want to empower a decentralized network of innovators to create value in the AI space and earn accordingly"

CASCDR Platform​

  • Decentralized AI platform built on Lightning Network
  • ChatGPT proxy via Lightning: sender privacy, anti-centralization, pay-as-you-go
  • Because it's on Lightning, AI agents can combine tools in series for complex tasks
