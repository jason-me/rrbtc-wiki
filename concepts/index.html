Concepts Overview

Core explanations of Bitcoin mechanisms, protocols, and foundational ideas, broken down so you can make informed decisions about your stack, from running a node to understanding privacy models.

📄️Accessibility in Bitcoin

Guidance on designing and evaluating Bitcoin wallets, apps, and workflows for universal usability across visual, cognitive, auditory, motor, and temporary impairments.

📄️AI and Bitcoin

Exploration of the intersection between artificial intelligence and Bitcoin, covering decentralized AI, Lightning payments for AI services, and the risks of AI centralization.

📄️Bitcoin Address Types

Comparison of Bitcoin address formats including Legacy, P2SH, SegWit (Bech32), native SegWit, and Taproot, with guidance on which to use for compatibility, privacy, and future-proofing.

📄️Bitcoin Education

Overview of Bitcoin education resources, curricula, and pedagogical approaches for developers, users, and educators across technical and non-technical audiences.

📄️Bitcoin Node Types

Comparison of full, pruned, archival, Simplified Payment Verification (SPV), and neutral nodes; explains trade-offs in storage, trust, privacy, and validation guarantees.

📄️Bitcoin Protocol

Survey of the Bitcoin protocol layer including consensus rules, peer-to-peer messaging, mempool policy, and the BIP process for proposing changes.

📄️Chaumian Ecash

Explanation of Chaumian eCash principles, blind signatures, and modern implementations like Cashu and Fedimint for private, custodial or federated ecash on Bitcoin.

📄️Circular Economy

Examination of earning, spending, and saving entirely in Bitcoin without converting to fiat, including merchant directories, gift cards, and peer-to-peer commerce tools.

📄️Custody Models

Comparison of self-custody, multisig, collaborative custody, and custodial solutions with threat-model guidance for choosing the right approach.

📄️Home Mining

Practical guide to solo and pooled mining at home using consumer hardware, hash marketplaces, and Stratum V2 / DATUM template control.

📄️Lightning Network

Overview of Lightning Network architecture, channel management, routing, Bolt 12 offers, and trade-offs versus on-chain and other Layer 2 solutions.

📄️Mesh Networks

Survey of mesh networking projects (GoTenna, LoRaWAN, Reticulum) for Bitcoin transaction propagation and resilience in disconnected or censored environments.

📄️Mining Payout Schemas

Comparison of PPLNS, FPPS, PPS+, TIDES, and solo mining payout models with variance, latency, and pool fee implications.

📄️Multisig

Deep dive into n-of-m multisignature schemes, hardware wallet coordination, deterministic wallets (BIP-85), and popular multisig coordinators (Sparrow, Nunchuk, CARAVAN).

📄️No-KYC Acquisition

Methods for acquiring Bitcoin without identity verification including Bisq, RoboSats, HodlHodl, Azteco vouchers, Bitcoin ATMs, and in-person trades.

📄️Nostr

Introduction to the Nostr protocol for censorship-resistant social networking, including clients, relays, NIPs, and integration with Lightning via Zaps.

📄️P2P Exchange

Comparison of peer-to-peer Bitcoin exchanges: Bisq, RoboSats, HodlHodl, LocalBitcoins (deprecated), and emerging decentralized order books.

📄️Payjoin

Explanation of PayJoin (BIP-78) interactive transactions for improved on-chain privacy, wallet support, and coordination with receiver collaboration.

📄️Privacy Stacks

Modular compositions of tools for on-chain, Lightning, and metadata privacy — including CoinJoin, Silent Payments, Tor, and Nostr/SSH isolation.

📄️Proof of Work

Fundamentals of Nakamoto consensus, difficulty adjustment, hash rate measurement, energy economics, and the security model underlying Bitcoin's immutability.

📄️UTXO Model

Explanation of the UTXO accounting model, coin selection, consolidation, dust limits, and how UTXO management affects privacy, fees, and wallet performance.
