You do not need to understand how any of this works. You just need to keep these items safe and hand them to [HEIR NAME] if and when they contact you.

What Is Inside Your Envelope​

  • A steel stamped metal plate with words on it — this is the seed phrase

How to Store It​

  • Do not store it near strong magnets or extreme heat
  • Do not loan it to anyone
  • Do not photograph the metal plate or the QR card

  • If you move home, bring it with you and let [HEIR NAME] know your new address

  3. Be available to help — they may ask you to meet in person or to be present while they complete a process. Please cooperate as best you can.
